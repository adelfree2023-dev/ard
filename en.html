About Us

Your Trusted Partner for Setting Up the Finest Events

"Al-Moatamad for Events and Weddings" was established to be the primary destination and the most trusted name in Qatar for setting up all official and private events. We do not just provide equipment; we paint a complete picture of luxury that suits your guests.

From wedding and national event tents to mourning and condolence tents, we are committed to providing the highest standards of service and speed in execution, with a wide range of the finest furniture, majlises, carpets, lighting, and modern air conditioning.

  • European tents resistant to harsh weather conditions.
  • Wide variety of traditional sofas, Sadu, suede, and luxury furnishings.
